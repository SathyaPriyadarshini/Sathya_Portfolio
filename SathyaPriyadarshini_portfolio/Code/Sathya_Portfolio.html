About Me

I’m an interaction designer passionate about creating inclusive, accessible, and culturally sensitive digital products. My work is driven by empathy and creativity, with a focus on simplifying complexity and connecting people through meaningful experiences.

I bring a thoughtful balance of human-centred design, research, and collaboration always aiming to make technology more approachable.

Outside of design, you’ll often find me at art exhibitions or design meet-ups. Continuous learning keeps me excited and growing as a designer.

My Learnings

Each project teaches me something new — here are the lessons that shape my design journey.

Curiosity
01

Ask as many questions as you can.

Curiosity uncovers insights and avoids assumptions.

Clarity
02

Define the problem well.

Clear problem statements focus effort on what matters.

Iterations
03

Iterate and seek feedback.

Continuous improvement builds thoughtful solutions.

Adaptability
04

Assess blockers and adapt.

Challenges are unique; pivot and keep moving.

Testing
05

Test UI in the real output.

Users see the coded product, not design files.

Balance
06

Mind business goals too.

Great design aligns user needs and business value.
